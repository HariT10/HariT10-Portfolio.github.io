about me

Hi, my name is Harishan Thilakanathan, and welcome to my portfolio website. This site showcases various projects, experiences, skills, and other noteworthy aspects of my life. My goal is to stay consistent and disciplined in developing and creating, and this website reflects that journey.

The Projects section highlights key software and coding projects I have worked on or am currently developing. It also includes an extended section featuring smaller projects and school assignments that I’ve particularly enjoyed working on.

I have experience with programming languages such as C, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and Python. Additionally, I am familiar with tools like GitHub, GitLab, Trello, WordPress, Linux environments, and the Adobe Suite. I also have experience with agile methodologies and team collaboration, thanks to the Software Design classes I have taken.

Outside of coding, I am passionate about soccer, basketball, going on runs, and love video creation, including video editing and filming.
